Nestle India. - One Page Profile

by Sheetal Singh ParmarMarch 2nd, 2024

For this project, I compiled a one-page company profile summarizing essential information about the company, including a brief overview, key financial metrics, financial ratios, shareholding pattern, managerial remuneration, capital structure, five-year volume and historical prices, and recent significant updates sourced from the company's Annual Report.

Using data from the Annual Report, I calculated various key financial metrics, financial ratios, and managerial remuneration of major shareholders, in addition to highlighting recent corporate actions undertaken by the company.

Key Objective

The primary objective of this one-page profile was to condense all important financial indicators of the company into a single page, providing a comprehensive snapshot of its current state.
